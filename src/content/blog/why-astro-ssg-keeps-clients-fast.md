---
title: Why Astro SSG keeps clients fast
description: The case for static delivery, edge caching, and content that loads before the user finishes the first scroll.
date: 2025-12-20
categoryId: build
---

Astro ships less JavaScript by default, which keeps first paint fast and interactions snappy. Static delivery also simplifies caching at the edge.

Combined with content that loads before the user reaches it, the site feels instant even on slower connections.
